The Titanic Disaster, 1912

In 1912 the Titanic was touted as The Unsinkable Ship.
Obviously history shows this was nonsense, as on 15th April 1912 the Titanic sank after hitting an iceberg in the North Atlantic ocean.

Titanic Belfast is the premier resource for Titanic information in the world, attracting thousands of tourists every year.
